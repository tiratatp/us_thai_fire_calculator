## 4. NIIT (IRC §1411)

Rate: 3.8% on lesser of (a) net investment income or (b) MAGI above threshold.

| Filing Status | MAGI Threshold |
|---------------|----------------|
| Single / HoH | $200,000 |
| MFJ | $250,000 |
| MFS | $125,000 |
| Estate/Trust (2025) | $15,650 |

Not inflation-adjusted.

**Source:** https://www.irs.gov/taxtopics/tc559

## 6. 10% Early-Withdrawal Penalty Exceptions (IRC §72(t))

| Exception | Applies | Detail |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| Age 59½ | IRA & plans | No penalty after 59½ |
| Rule of 55 | 401(k)/403(b) only | Separation in year turning 55+ (age 50 for public safety) |
| 72(t) SEPP | IRA & plans | 3 methods; locked ≥5 yr or to 59½; one-time switch to RMD method |
| Roth basis | Roth IRA | Contributions withdrawable anytime tax/penalty-free |
| Unreimbursed medical | IRA & plans | >7.5% of AGI |
| First-time homebuyer | IRA only | $10k lifetime |
| Higher education | IRA only | Qualified expenses |
| Disability/Death | IRA & plans | — |
| Health ins while unemployed | IRA only | 12 consecutive weeks UI |
| Birth/adoption | IRA & plans | $5k per child |

**Source:** https://www.irs.gov/retirement-plans/plan-participant-employee/retirement-topics-exceptions-to-tax-on-early-distributions

## 7. Roth 5-Year Rules

| Rule | Detail |
|------|--------|
| (a) Qualified distribution | Roth IRA open ≥5 tax years starting Jan 1 of first contribution year + age 59½ (or death/disability) → earnings tax/penalty-free |
| (b) Per-conversion 5-year | Each conversion has own 5-yr clock for 10% penalty on converted principal if withdrawn before 59½ |

Ordering: (1) Contributions → (2) Conversions FIFO → (3) Earnings.

**Source:** https://www.irs.gov/publications/p590b

## 8. HSA Rules

2025 limits: Self $4,300 / Family $8,550 / Age 55+ +$1,000 catch-up.

| Feature | Pre-65 | Post-65 |
|---------|--------|---------|
| Non-qualified withdrawal | 20% penalty + income tax | Ordinary income tax only, no penalty |
| Qualified medical | Tax-free | Tax-free |
| Contributions | Requires HDHP, no Medicare | Must stop on Medicare enrollment |
| RMD | None | None |

**Source:** https://www.irs.gov/publications/p969
